Login Credentials & Support

All users (staff, parents, and students) access Mastex SchoolOS with usernames and passwords that you or your admin team assign when registering them. There is no public registration or automatic account creation.

  • Use “Add Staff”, “Manage Parents”, and “Add Student” to create and update user accounts.
  • If a user forgets their username or password, they should contact the school office. You can then look up their account and change their password safely.
  • For new schools joining the platform, the platform super admin creates the first school admin account and shares the login credentials securely.
