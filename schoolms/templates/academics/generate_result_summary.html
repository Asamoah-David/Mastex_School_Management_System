📊 Generate Result Summaries

This will calculate and store the final scores for all students based on their CA and exam scores.

{# Info Box #}

How it works:

  1. System collects all CA scores and calculates average (weighted at {{ policy.ca_weight }}%)
  2. System retrieves the exam score (weighted at {{ policy.exam_weight }}%)
  3. Final score = (CA × {{ policy.ca_weight }}%) + (Exam × {{ policy.exam_weight }}%)
  4. Grade and GPA are assigned based on the final score
