## 1. What is Mastex SchoolOS?

Mastex SchoolOS is a **web-based school management platform**. It helps your school:

- Register and manage **students**, **parents**, and **staff**
- Run **academics**: timetables, homework, results, report cards, exams, online classes
- Manage **money**: school fees, optional services (canteen, transport, hostel, textbooks), expenses, budgets
- Track **attendance** and **absence requests**
- Run **operations**: library, admissions, announcements, events, certificates, ID cards, and more
- **Communicate**: in-app notifications, SMS (where configured), and internal messaging

Fee screens typically use **GHS** (Ghana Cedis). **Paystack** powers online card payments when your school configures it in **School settings**.

Your school may turn features on or off. If a menu item is missing, it may be disabled for your school or not allowed for your **role**.

## 2. Getting started

### 2.1 Browser and access

Use a recent **Chrome**, **Edge**, **Firefox**, or **Safari**. Your school gives you the **website address** (for example `https://your-school.example.com`).

### 2.2 Signing in

1. Open the site and choose **Sign in** (or go to `/accounts/login/`).
2. Enter the **email** and **password** your school provided.
3. You land on a **dashboard** suited to your role.

Use **password reset** on the login page when your school has email configured.

### 2.3 Signing out, profile, search

- **Log out** from the profile menu (top right).
- **My profile** / **Edit profile** / **Change password** are in the same menu.
- **Search** in the header (when shown) finds students, fees, or other records your role may access.

### 2.4 Sidebar navigation

On large screens, the **sidebar** groups features into sections you can expand or collapse. On phones, open the **menu** to reach the same links.

### 2.5 Public admission (no login)

Visitors can often **Apply** for admission and **Track** an application from the home page when your school enables those links.

### 2.6 First-week checklist (implementation team)

1. Put the school **URL** on every parent letter and notice board.
2. Headteacher (or delegate): **School settings** — logo, academic year.
3. Bursar: **Fee structures** and a test walkthrough with one volunteer family.
4. ICT: create test **teacher** and **parent** accounts; confirm timetable, homework, and notifications.
5. Send a short message: “Use [URL] with the email we have on file; use Forgot password if needed.”

## 3. Notifications and SMS

### 3.1 In-app notifications

The **bell** in the header shows recent alerts; **View all** opens the full list (`/notifications/`).

You may be notified when, for example:

- A **result** is posted or updated (parent)
- A **fee** is assigned or a **payment** is recorded (parent)
- An **absence request** is approved or rejected
- The school publishes **announcements**, **calendar** entries, or **events** for your audience

Very large announcements may be **throttled** so the system stays reliable for everyone; limits are configured by **Mastex Technologies** or your school’s technical partner — not by parents or teachers.

### 3.2 SMS

Optional **SMS** (fees, admissions, etc.) uses **mNotify** when your school has configured it with help from **Mastex Technologies**. Some SMS types can be turned off while leaving others on — your leadership decides policy; Mastex Technologies applies the technical settings.

## 6. Questions boards and bursars ask

- **Do we install an app?** No — browser on phone or computer; “Add to Home Screen” is optional.
- **Is our data only our school’s?** Yes — users see your school’s workspace, not other schools’ records.
- **Can we start with only fees and students?** Yes — many schools phase in attendance, homework, library, and services.
- **How do online payments reach us?** Through **Paystack** and your bank arrangement; finance reconciles using **payment dashboard** and fee lists.

## 8. User roles (overview)

Each user has a **primary role**. Some have **secondary roles** (e.g. teacher + librarian).

| Role | Typical purpose |
|------|-----------------|
| **Headteacher / School Admin** | Full management of your school (people, academics, finance, operations) |
| **Deputy Head / HOD** | Leadership subset: people, academics, attendance, discipline, some finance |
| **Teacher** | Classes, attendance, homework, results, exams, library, messages |
| **Accountant / Bursar** | Fees, payments, expenses, budgets, financial reports |
| **Librarian** | Library catalog, issues, management |
| **Admission Officer** | Applications pipeline, students list, services hub |
| **School Nurse** | Health records and visits |
| **Admin Assistant** | Staff/parent/student lists, documents, services hub, announcements |
| **Staff** | Directory, students, services hub, timetable, attendance support |
| **Parent** | Children’s academics, fees, services, absence, PT meetings (if enabled), messages |
| **Student** | Timetable, homework, results, fees, library, exams, absence requests |

If an action is **forbidden** or a menu is **missing**, check role and school feature settings.

## 21. Tips and good practice

1. Keep **phone and email** accurate on every profile.
2. **One account per person** — separate parent logins instead of shared passwords.
3. Check **notifications** after fees or absence actions.
4. Use **Services hub** for optional services so balances stay coherent.
5. If a bulk message seems cut short, ask your **administrator** or **Mastex Technologies** instead of spamming retries.
